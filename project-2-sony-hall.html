Sony Hall

Improving the user experience on social media for a music venue in NYC.

SUMMARY

Sony Hall is a new music venue in NYC that needed a more defined brand and online presence. I refined and implemented a brand voice and social media strategy to improve their followers’ experience online.

ROLES

User Research
Branding
Graphic Design

TOOLS

Adobe Photoshop
Planoly
Hootsuite

The Problem

Initially, Sony Hall had a little over 4,000 followers and very low engagement on Instagram. They needed more interesting and compelling content to connect with followers and get people excited about their venue.

The Solution

I created a content strategy plan to increase engagement and gain more followers for Sony Hall.

The main things we focused on were:

  • Curating visual content from past shows, vintage photos of the venue and NYC, and pop culture references
  • Excluding admat posters with too much text
  • Diligent community management to improve customer service with online followers

View Strategy Plan

Implementing the Strategy

Since the venue had a very small budget for Instagram promotions, we started with giveaway promotions to increase engagement and following without spending money. We executed giveaway contests regularly and I created unique graphics for each one using photos of the venue taken by fans.

Conclusion

After 6 months, Sony Hall reached their goal of 10K followers on Instagram and they saw an increase in engagement. They also began developing meaningful relationships with their fans and concert attendees by interacting with them daily.
